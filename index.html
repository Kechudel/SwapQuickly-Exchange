Frequently Asked Questions

It's simple: contact us on WhatsApp or email, tell us which coin you're sending and what you want back, confirm the quote and wallet we give you, then send your crypto. Once the blockchain confirms your transaction, we send your swapped coins immediately.

Most swaps complete within 15–45 minutes. This depends on blockchain confirmation time for the coin you're sending. Bitcoin typically takes longer (3–6 confirmations), while Solana, TRON, and BNB are usually much faster.

Yes, SwapQ operates as a P2P (peer-to-peer) exchange. This means you're swapping directly with us — no third-party intermediary, no automated liquidity pool. We handle the swap personally, which allows for faster resolution and human support on every transaction.

We support Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Solana (SOL), BNB (BNB Chain), Cardano (ADA), TRON (TRX), Litecoin (LTC), and USDT on both TRC20 and ERC20 networks. More assets are being added — ask us if yours isn't listed.

We charge a small service fee that's already baked into the rate we quote you. There are no surprise charges. The rate you see is the rate you get. The only additional cost is the standard blockchain transaction fee (network fee) for sending your coins.

We operate transparently and personally. Every swap is handled by our team, not an algorithm. We provide a wallet address verified for each transaction, and we only release your coins after confirming receipt on the blockchain. We recommend starting with a smaller test swap if it's your first time.

Reach out immediately via WhatsApp or email. Since every swap involves a real person on our end, issues are resolved quickly. Blockchain transactions are irreversible, so double-check wallet addresses before sending — our support team is always available to help troubleshoot.
